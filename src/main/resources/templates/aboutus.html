The Project

Hawker food culture is an integral part of Singapore's culinary heritage, with a diverse range of local dishes and flavors that reflect the nation's multiculturalism. The COVID-19 pandemic has highlighted the importance of an online presence for food hawkers in Singapore. With social distancing measures in place and a decrease in foot traffic at physical locations, many food hawkers have had to adapt to survive.

We hope to encourage more food hawkers to have an online presence to reach a wider audience and increase their visibility in the competitive food industry. As consumers are increasingly turning to online platforms to discover new food options and make purchases, having an online presence will allow food hawkers to showcase their offerings, share their stories, and connect with customers beyond their physical location.
